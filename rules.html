=============== Rose SMP Rules ===============

1: Don't be toxic or hateful

This is an over-decade old version of a block game; don't be a jerk here.


2: Don't grief or steal.

If it's not yours, don't take or modify it without permission.
A player is considered incactive after 30 days of not logging on, and thus any very unfinished builds of theirs may be modified or used, given that it's not being straight-up destroyed.
If a town has a rule that lowers this threshold, it may not be enforced retroactively.


3: No excessive arguing.

We try to keep this server as drama-free as possible, so don't instigate/drag on arguments or be rude. If you're told to stop an argument by staff, do so.


4: No hacking or using exploits.

Don't do this, it makes the server less fun and fair for everyone.


5: Don't spam or be intentionally annoying.

Again, we try to be drama-free and play the block game.


6: No NSFW.

Don't make grahpic sex jokes, distribute nsfw material, etc. Keep in mind minors play here.
